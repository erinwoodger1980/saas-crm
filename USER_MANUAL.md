## üë§ **Leads Management**

### **What are Leads?**
Leads are potential customers who have enquired about your services. They move through different stages from initial enquiry to won/lost.

### **Lead Stages**
- **New Enquiry**: Fresh leads that need review
- **Info Requested**: Waiting for more details from customer
- **Disqualified**: Not a good fit for your business
- **Rejected**: Customer declined or not interested
- **Ready to Quote**: All information gathered, ready for pricing
- **Quote Sent**: Proposal has been sent to customer
- **Won**: Customer accepted your quote
- **Lost**: Customer went with someone else

### **How to Create a Lead**
1. Go to **Leads** section
2. Click **"New Lead"** button
3. Fill in contact details
4. Add description of the enquiry
5. Save the lead

### **Lead Actions**
- **Request Info**: Send questionnaire to customer
- **Request Supplier Quote**: Get pricing from suppliers
- **Move to Quote Builder**: Create customer proposal
- **Delete Lead**: Permanently remove (be careful!)

### **Import Leads from CSV**
1. Go to **Leads** section
2. Click **"üìä Import CSV"** button
3. Upload your CSV file
4. Map columns to lead fields
5. Import leads in bulk

## üí∞ **Opportunities & Sales Pipeline**

### **What are Opportunities?**
Opportunities are leads that have progressed to potential sales. They track the likelihood of winning and expected value.

### **Opportunity Stages**
- **Prospect**: Early stage, gathering requirements
- **Proposal**: Quote has been sent
- **Negotiation**: Discussing terms and pricing
- **Won**: Deal closed successfully
- **Lost**: Deal didn't proceed

### **Managing Opportunities**
1. Opportunities are automatically created when leads reach "Quote Sent" stage
2. Update probability and expected close date
3. Track deal value and progress
4. Mark as Won/Lost when deal concludes

## üíº **Quote Builder**

### **Creating Customer Quotes**
1. From a lead, click **"Move to Quote Builder"**
2. Add line items manually or parse supplier PDFs
3. Set your markup/margin percentage
4. Add terms and conditions
5. Generate PDF proposal for customer

### **Supplier Quote Management**
1. Upload supplier PDF quotes
2. Click **"Parse supplier PDFs"** to extract line items
3. Review and adjust extracted items
4. Apply your markup to create customer pricing
5. Generate professional customer proposal

### **Quote Sections**
- **Header**: Customer details and quote reference
- **Line Items**: Products/services with quantities and pricing
- **Totals**: Subtotal, VAT, and final total
- **Terms**: Payment terms and conditions
- **Footer**: Company information and contact details
